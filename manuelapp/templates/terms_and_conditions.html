2. Reservation System

Our reservation system features an interactive calendar that displays the availability of condos. Dates marked in green on the calendar are available for booking, while dates marked in red are unavailable.

Available Dates: Indicated in green on the calendar.

Unavailable Dates: Indicated in red on the calendar.
